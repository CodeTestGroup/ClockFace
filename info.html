About ClockFace

This page allows you to adjust the settings of your clock. You can change the background color, text color, choose between a 12-hour or 24-hour format, adjust the clock size, and switch between a digital or analog clock style!

All settings are stored in cookies, so they persist even after you refresh or revisit the page
